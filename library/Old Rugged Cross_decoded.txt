Old Rugged Cross

[Verse 1]

On a hill far away stood
an old rugged cross

The emblem of
suff'ring and shame

And I love that old cross

Where the dearest and best

For a world of lost sinners was slain

[chorus]

So I'll cherish the old rugged cross

'Till my trophies at last I lay down

I will cling to the old rugged cross

And exchange it someday for a crown

[Verse]

Atas bukit jauh nampak salib kayu

Lambang d'rita, sengsara, nista

Yesus t'lah dipaku pada salib itu

Mati ganti insan berdosa

[Chorus]

Kan kukasihi t'rus salib-Nya

Sampai kutinggalkan yang fana

Akan kupegang t'rus salib-Nya

Sampai kutukarkan mahkota
