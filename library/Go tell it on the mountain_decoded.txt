[Verse 1]

menjaga dombanya

cahaya mulia

Hai siarkan di gunung

di bukit dan dimana jua

ketika mendengar

gempita mendengar

Go tell it on the mountain

over the hills and everywhere

Go tell it on the mountain

That Jesus Christ is born

[Ending]
